## рџ“– Overview

**AquaSetu** is an AI-powered web platform designed to modernize canal water allocation by assisting irrigation authorities with transparent, explainable, and adaptive decision support.

Traditional canal irrigation systems often rely on fixed schedules that do not adapt to changing rainfall, water availability, crop requirements, or seasonal demand. AquaSetu addresses these challenges by providing intelligent allocation recommendations while keeping government officials in complete control of final decisions.

Alongside water allocation, AquaSetu also provides an integrated **AI Crop & Irrigation Advisor** that helps farmers make informed agricultural decisions related to irrigation, crop planning, and sustainable farming.

---

# рџЋЇ Problem Statement

Many irrigation systems continue to operate using manual or static water distribution schedules that cannot adapt to changing environmental conditions.

This leads to:

- Water wastage
- Unequal distribution
- Farmer disputes
- Reduced agricultural productivity
- Lack of transparency
- Difficult decision-making during water scarcity

AquaSetu provides an explainable AI-powered decision support platform that helps governments distribute water more efficiently and fairly.
