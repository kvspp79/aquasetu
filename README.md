# 🌊 AquaSetu

> **Explainable AI Decision Intelligence Platform for Adaptive Canal Water Allocation**

AquaSetu is a government-ready AI-powered decision support platform that helps irrigation authorities allocate canal water fairly, transparently, and sustainably. By combining optimization, explainable AI, fairness-aware allocation, and decision intelligence, AquaSetu assists canal officers in making informed water distribution decisions while keeping humans in control.

---

## 📖 Overview

Water allocation in many canal irrigation systems still relies on fixed schedules that do not adapt to changing rainfall, reservoir levels, crop demands, or water scarcity. This often leads to disputes, inefficient distribution, and reduced agricultural productivity.

AquaSetu modernizes this process by providing **AI-generated allocation recommendations** that consider real-time conditions, fairness, sustainability, and policy constraints. The final decision always remains with the responsible government authority.
